About Roy Tours

Welcome to Roy Tours — your trusted partner in exploring the soul of India. We are not just a travel company; we are your local friend who takes you beyond sightseeing into the heart of every destination. Whether it’s a peaceful village retreat, a vibrant cultural experience, or a nature-filled escape, we create journeys that stay with you forever.

Our Origin: Roy Tours was born with a simple dream — to make travel affordable, authentic, and accessible to everyone. Coming from a humble background, our founder Bhaskar Kumar always believed that tourism shouldn’t be limited to cities and five-star hotels. He wanted to bring people closer to real India — the villages, the people, the traditions, and the untouched beauty.

Our Mission: We aim to empower rural tourism while giving travelers meaningful experiences. Every trip with Roy Tours supports local communities, promotes eco-friendly practices, and builds cultural bridges between travelers and locals.

What Makes Us Different?

  • Authentic Village Experiences: Stay with local families, participate in festivals, visit organic farms, and learn traditional crafts.
  • Personalized Travel: No fixed templates — we curate every trip based on your interests, budget, and travel goals.
  • Affordable and Transparent: No hidden costs. We believe in simple, honest pricing.
  • Local Empowerment: 80% of what you pay directly goes to local hosts, guides, and artisans.

Our Team: We’re a passionate group of local guides, techies, artists, storytellers, and nature lovers. From our tour planners to your village hosts — everyone is focused on making your journey comfortable and unforgettable.

Safety & Comfort: All our trips are planned keeping your safety and comfort in mind. Verified homestays, hygienic food, and 24/7 support — so you can travel worry-free.

Join Us On This Journey: Whether you're a solo traveler looking for peace, a couple seeking romance, or a group of friends craving adventure — Roy Tours has something special for everyone. Let us take you away from the usual and into the extraordinary.

Thank you for believing in small dreams that make a big difference. Welcome to the Roy Tours family — where your journey is personal, and your memories are everlasting.
